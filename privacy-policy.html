Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Socialmix handles information when it is used to prepare and publish video content to a Facebook Page.

Information We Process

Socialmix may process video files, generated clips, subtitles, transcript text, Facebook Page identifiers, and access tokens that are configured by the app operator.

How Information Is Used

Information is used only to operate the content workflow, including uploading approved clips to the Facebook Page selected by the app operator.

Facebook Permissions

Socialmix uses Facebook permissions only for Page publishing and Page management actions explicitly initiated by the app operator. It does not sell Facebook data or use it for advertising profiling.

Storage

Socialmix is self-hosted. Files, tokens, and database records are stored in the app operator's own deployment environment. The operator controls retention, backups, and deletion.

Sharing

Socialmix sends approved video clips and descriptions to Meta services only when the operator chooses to publish to a Facebook Page. It does not share data with unrelated third parties.

Data Deletion

See the User Data Deletion page for instructions to remove app data and revoke Facebook access.

Contact

For privacy or deletion requests, contact the app operator who manages the Socialmix deployment and the connected Facebook Page.
